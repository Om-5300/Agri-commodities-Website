About Sesame Seeds

S.K. AGRI FOOD provides high-quality sesame seeds throughout the world. Sesame, a flowering plant in the genus Sesame, commonly known as “Till,” is one of the oldest cultivated plants in the world. Sesame seeds are used to add texture and flavor to a variety of rolls, crackers, salad dressings, bakery & confectionery, candy making, production of Tahini, and Sesame Oil (which is used for cooking, manufacturing perfumes, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetic products).

Natural Sesame Seeds contain all the natural nutrients of sesame seeds and are a rich source of edible oil. They are counted among the most nutritious seeds due to their protein and valuable amino acid content. India is one of the largest producers and exporters of Sesame seeds.

Black Sesame Seeds are rich in healthy omega-3 fatty acids that help lubricate the intestinal walls and aid in digestion. They also have a slightly nuttier, bitterer flavor compared to their sweeter white counterparts.

Hulled Sesame Seeds are sesame seeds from which the outer covering or hulls have been removed. They have a shiny pearl-white appearance and contain more Vitamin A than non-hulled varieties.
