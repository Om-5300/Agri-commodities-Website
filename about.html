About Us

S.K. AGRI FOOD is a fast-growing exporter of agro-products from India. With a team of experienced professionals, we ensure high-quality products at competitive prices. We have successfully exported numerous products to hundreds of buyers worldwide, becoming the number one choice for our customers.

Our expertise includes a diverse range of agro-products such as cumin, fennel, fenugreek, mustard, sesame, coriander, spices, grains, animal feeds, oil seeds, and flour. Partnering with quality suppliers across the country, we provide only the best to our customers.

Our Services

We manage both vessel loads and container loads of bulk agricultural commodities, with options for standard and organic products. Our exclusive export team ensures quality, timely shipments, and customer satisfaction.

Our warehouse near the city enables efficient processing and shipment, while our advanced technologies allow us to handle bulk quantities quickly and effectively.
